    How to Use the Finance Tracker

    1. Enter a short description of your transaction (e.g., "Salary" or "Groceries").
    2. Enter the amount in Indian Rupees. Use + for income and - for expenses.
    3. Click "Add Transaction" to save it. It will appear in the history list below.
    4. Your balance, total income, and total expenses will automatically update.
    5. A pie chart gives you a visual summary of your finances.
    6. To remove an entry, click the ‚ùå button next to it.
    7. All your data is saved in your browser, even if you refresh the page!
